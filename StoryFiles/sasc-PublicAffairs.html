Student Academic Success Centre

Turns out the Student Academic Success Centre is associated with Learning Support Services, but also has other services that could come in handy. For example, at the Advising Centre here in Tory, you can drop in to get advice on issues from understanding academic regulations to developing a plan to get your studies back on track. There are even a few Peer Assisted Study Sessions for Public Affairs students.

With all these resources available to you, you start to feel pretty confident about your ability to succeed at this whole university thing. You decide to check out Career Services at 401 Tory because, as they say, it's never to early to be thinking about your future!
